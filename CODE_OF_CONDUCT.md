# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

QR-SHIELD is committed to fostering a respectful, inclusive community of security researchers, engineers, and professionals. We are dedicated to providing a harassment-free environment for all participants, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behaviors

- Being respectful of differing opinions and approaches

- Providing and accepting constructive criticism gracefully

- Focusing on what is best for the community

- Showing empathy toward other community members

- Being patient and supporting newcomers

- Using inclusive language and terminology

- Contributing to discussions with clear, evidence-based perspectives

- Acknowledging the work and contributions of others

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind

- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics

- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances

- Deliberate misgendering or use of dead names

- Public or private harassment

- Publishing private information without consent

- Threats or incitement to violence

- Conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

- Bad faith arguments or trolling

- Spam or continuous off-topic discussion

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including:

- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions)

- Project documentation and comments

- Community forums and communication channels

- Project events and meetings

- Any official project communication

This Code of Conduct also applies when representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. Violations should be reported privately.

### Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation:

1. **Document the incident** - Save relevant messages or context

1. **Report privately** - Contact the project maintainer directly

1. **Provide details** - Include dates, participants, and description

1. **Allow investigation** - Maintain confidentiality during review

**Reporting Contact:** dreamdrafted000@gmail.com

### Investigation

Reports will be:

- Acknowledged within 48 hours

- Investigated fairly and thoroughly

- Handled confidentially

- Resolved within a reasonable timeframe

### Consequences

Violations may result in:

- Temporary or permanent ban from project spaces

- Removal of contributions

- Escalation to law enforcement if appropriate

- Public statement if warranted

## Specific Guidelines for This Project

### Research Ethics

This project focuses on security research. When discussing or contributing:

- Always emphasize defensive and research applications

- Never promote unauthorized access, illegal activity, or unsafe testing practices

- Respect legal and ethical boundaries

- Consider the broader impact of discussions

- Support responsible disclosure practices

### Technical Discussions

- Base arguments on evidence and testing

- Acknowledge uncertainty and limitations

- Respect different expertise levels

- Welcome different approaches

- Focus on technical merit

### Community Support

- Welcome newcomers and help them get started

- Answer questions patiently

- Provide constructive feedback

- Celebrate others' contributions

- Support diverse participation

## Moderation

### Moderation Actions

Community leaders may:

- Request clarification of intent

- Request cessation of inappropriate behavior

- Remove offensive content

- Warn violators of consequences

- Temporarily or permanently ban individuals

### Appeals

Individuals who believe a moderation decision was unfair may:

1. Request explanation of the decision

1. Submit written appeal within 7 days

1. Participate in review process

1. Accept final decision by maintainer
